2016 Graduate Student Conference in Logic

University of Notre Dame

The 17th annual Graduate Student Conference in Logic will be held at the University of Notre Dame on Saturday, April 9 and Sunday, April 10 2016. This conference is intended for graduate students in mathematical logic. Graduate students will give contributed talks in multiple sessions, including sessions on model theory, computability, and other topics to suit demand. Drs. Gabriel Conant and Rutger Kuyper will give the keynote addresses.

The conference schedule is available here.

Notre Dame students Greg Cousins, Mike Haskel, and Rose Weisshaar are organizing the conference. Please direct any inquiries to mhaskel@nd.edu.

This conference is made possible by generous funding from Notre Dame's Research and Training Grant in logic from the National Science Foundation, from the Association for Symbolic Logic, and from the Notre Dame Graduate Student Union.
